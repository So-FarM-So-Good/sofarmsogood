What We Do

SoFarmSoGood is a farm friendly software that intends to encourage farmers to produce and sell their harvest. In Turkey, sugar beet producers face with quota problem.Encouraging local farmers of a harvest into whole purchasing system is an undeniably important issue for agriculture supported economy.We offer secure, robust and scalable blockchain system, which is named SoFarmSoGood.In our solution, we have three main actors; farmer, cooperative, company. Through virtual cooperatives we offer an innovative solution to local farmers for passing the quota limitation. Thus, more local farmers can become part of production chain.We always need to remember, having happier and more welfare farmers enables happy agriculture and having happy agriculture enables national welfare and happiness.
